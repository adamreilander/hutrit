## Visual Direction Notes (for design team)

- **Image concept:** Bold, clean, aspirational. Could show a professional at a laptop in a home office — confident, not stressed. Or a strong typographic layout with the key line "Tu talento vale más de lo que te están pagando."
- **Color palette:** Hutrit Club brand colors. Dark background with strong contrast works well for this message.
- **Text overlay (if used):** "Tu talento vale más." — large, centered, high contrast.
- **Format:** 1:1 or 4:5 for feed. 9:16 if repurposed as a Reel/Story cover.
- **No stock-photo "happy freelancer" imagery** — this audience is skeptical. Use real, grounded visuals.

---

## Quality Review Notes

- **Hook** leads with identity + frustration in one line — immediately recognizable to the target audience
- **Body** names specific actions they do (calificar, concertar) — shows this content was written for them, not a generic audience
- **Offer statement** is concrete: role names, format (remote), compensation (USD), process (curated)
- **Barrier reduction:** "No una lista de vacantes. No promesas vagas." — directly counters scam fatigue
- **CTA** is double-layered: primary (apply now) + viral (tag a friend) — supports both conversion and reach
- **Hashtag strategy:** Mix of high-volume (#TrabajoRemoto, #LATAM) with niche (#AppointmentSetter, #LeadQualifier) and geo-targeted (#Venezuela, #Bolivia, #Peru, #Ecuador)
- **Tone check:** Motivational without being manipulative. Empathetic without being patronizing. Direct without being cold.
- **Length:** ~200 words in body — optimal for Instagram (enough to tell a story, not so long it loses attention)
