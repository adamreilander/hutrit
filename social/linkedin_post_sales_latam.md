## Quality Review Notes

- Hook names the action (selling, qualifying) before naming the problem — grabs attention from sales professionals immediately
- Middle section names the pain without being dramatic ("no es un problema de talento")
- Process section is concrete and scannable — addresses the "too complicated/scam" barrier
- CTA is frictionless ("gratis, en minutos") and forward-looking
- Peer-sharing CTA at the end extends organic reach
- Tone is professional and direct — appropriate for LinkedIn without being cold or corporate
- Length: ~280 words — appropriate for LinkedIn organic (optimal engagement range: 200–400 words)
