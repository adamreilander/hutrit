# Campaign Brief: Sales Talent Recruitment — LATAM
**Brand:** Hutrit Club (B2C)
**Campaign name:** "Tu talento merece más" (Your talent deserves more)
**Date:** March 2026
**Status:** Active

---

## 1. Campaign Objective

Recruit high-caliber sales professionals in Venezuela, Bolivia, Peru, and Ecuador for remote roles as:
- **Appointment Setters** (Concertadores de Citas)
- **Lead Qualifiers** (Calificadores de Clientes Potenciales)

serving international companies through Hutrit Club.

**Primary KPI:** Qualified applications / profile sign-ups from target countries
**Secondary KPIs:** Click-through rate on paid/organic content, cost-per-application, show-up rate to initial screening

---

## 2. Audience ICP (Ideal Candidate Profile)

### Core Profile
- **Age:** 24–38
- **Location:** Venezuela, Bolivia, Peru, Ecuador (urban centers)
- **Experience:** 2–7 years in outbound sales, inside sales, telemarketing, customer success, or commercial roles
- **English level:** B1–B2 minimum (B2+ preferred for Lead Qualifier roles)
- **Tech comfort:** CRM tools (HubSpot, Salesforce or equivalent), video calls, Google Workspace
- **Current situation:** Employed locally with salary ceiling, freelancing informally, or actively seeking remote work

### Psychographic Profile
- Commercially skilled, self-motivated, and resilient
- Frustrated by local salary ceilings and lack of access to international opportunities
- Skeptical of fraudulent remote job offers (high scam fatigue, especially Venezuela)
- Strong aspiration for professional validation and international-level career growth
- Responds to proof, transparency, and peer community
- Wants to be taken seriously, not just processed

### Pain Points (Ranked by Intensity)
1. Earning in local currency / below market value for their skills
2. No visible, trustworthy pathway into international remote work
3. Prior bad experiences with scam offers
4. Imposter syndrome about English proficiency and international readiness
5. Lack of community and support in the remote job search

### Motivations (Ranked by Drive)
1. USD / hard currency income
2. Professional growth and international career trajectory
3. Remote flexibility and autonomy
4. Validation and belonging (community)
5. Process clarity and legitimate opportunity

## 4. Value Proposition for this Audience

### Core Value Proposition (Full Version)
Hutrit Club is the curated platform that bridges the gap between talented LATAM sales professionals and international companies that are actively hiring — through a structured, transparent process that validates your skills, places you in high-quality remote roles (Appointment Setter / Lead Qualifier), and supports your growth into a long-term international sales career.

### Value Proposition Pillars for this Campaign

| Pillar | What it means for this audience |
|---|---|
| **Oportunidades reales** | Positions from actual companies that are hiring — not listings that go nowhere |
| **Proceso claro** | You know exactly what happens at each step. No ghosting, no ambiguity |
| **Compensación en USD** | Real dollar-denominated income — the most powerful motivator |
| **Crecimiento profesional** | These roles are a launching pad — not a dead end |
| **Comunidad LATAM** | You're not alone — you join a network of professionals on the same path |

## 5. Tone Direction

### Overall Tone for this Campaign
**Motivational + Direct + Empathetic**

- Speaks directly to the frustration without being dramatic
- Uses "tú" form — close, personal, peer-level
- Avoids corporate-speak or recruitment jargon
- Does not over-promise; instead, demonstrates with structure and proof
- Balances aspiration ("you deserve better") with realism ("here's exactly how it works")
- Never condescending — treats the audience as capable professionals who need access, not charity

### Tone by Channel
| Channel | Tone Adjustment |
|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Professional, aspirational, thought-leadership adjacent — speaks to career identity |
| Instagram | Closer, more motivational, visually energetic — speaks to frustration and transformation |
| Landing Page | Consultative, clear, conversion-focused — answers every objection, shows process |

### Words to Use
- Oportunidades reales / Empresas reales
- Proceso claro / Transparente
- USD / Compensación internacional
- Tu próximo nivel
- Talento LATAM
- Concertar, calificar, crecer
- Curado, validado, acompañado

### Words to Avoid
- "Fácil dinero" / "Ganar desde casa sin experiencia"
- "Reclutamiento masivo"
- "Plataforma" (use "comunidad" or "red" instead when speaking to talent)
- "Vacante" (too transactional — use "oportunidad" or "rol")
- Overly corporate English terms without translation or context

## 6. Copy Pillars (by Channel)

### Pillar 1 — Identity (Who you are)
*"Eres un profesional de ventas con experiencia real. Ya puedes competir a nivel internacional."*
Use on: LinkedIn, Instagram, Landing page hero
Purpose: Validate self-image, establish that they already have what it takes

### Pillar 2 — Frustration (What's holding you back)
*"El problema no es tu talento. Es el mercado local que no puede pagarte lo que vales."*
Use on: Instagram, LinkedIn hook lines, Landing page pain section
Purpose: Name the pain explicitly — earns emotional trust

### Pillar 3 — Opportunity (What's possible)
*"Roles de Appointment Setter y Lead Qualifier para empresas internacionales. En remoto. Pagados en USD."*
Use on: All channels — this is the core offer statement
Purpose: Clearly communicate the role, format, and compensation

### Pillar 4 — Process (How it works)
*"Crea tu perfil → Pasa nuestro proceso → Empieza a trabajar con empresas reales."*
Use on: Landing page, Instagram carousel (if visual), LinkedIn body
Purpose: Reduce the "this is complicated/a scam" barrier with simplicity and clarity

### Pillar 5 — Community (You're not alone)
*"Únete a una red de profesionales LATAM que ya están trabajando con empresas internacionales."*
Use on: Landing page social proof, Instagram, CTA reinforcement
Purpose: Reduce isolation anxiety; make the decision feel lower-risk socially

### Pillar 6 — CTA (What to do now)
*"Crea tu perfil gratis hoy. El próximo paso en tu carrera empieza aquí."*
Use on: All channels — closing line / button text
Purpose: Friction-low, forward-looking action trigger

## 8. Notes for Execution

- Always distinguish Appointment Setter vs. Lead Qualifier roles — they attract slightly different profiles and the copy should allow both to self-identify
- Venezuela audience requires extra trust-building signals (scam fatigue is highest here)
- Peru audience requires differentiation from BPO/call center positioning (they want to "graduate" from that world)
- Bolivia audience benefits most from access/door-opening messaging (they haven't had pathways)
- Ecuador audience responds to growth and ambition framing (the USD advantage is smaller; career growth is the bigger hook)
- All content should be 100% in Spanish for this campaign — English only in role names where internationally recognized
