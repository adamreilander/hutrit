First confirmed Meta Ads performance benchmarks for Hutrit B2B campaigns (result type: Landing Page Visits).

**Period**: Feb 28 – Mar 29, 2026 (29 days)
**Campaigns analyzed**: 14 (3 active, 11 inactive at report date)
**Total spend**: €277.99 | **Total results**: 17,323 | **Total reach**: 260,942

**CPR Benchmarks (Landing Page Visits)**:
- Excellent: <= €0.012 (Sales Dev 2 at €0.0094, Campaña IA at €0.0121)
- Good: €0.012 – €0.016 (Sales Development at €0.0128, Director Comercial at €0.0109)
- Fair: €0.016 – €0.022 (Campaña Tech nueva, Project Manager, Talentoa, SEO)
- Poor: > €0.022 (Cyber, Automation Expert, Apolo, South Africa TECH)
- Anomaly/outlier: Paid Media at €0.0417 — likely misconfigured

**Blended CPR**: €0.0161
**Blended CTI (CPM)**: €0.670
**CTR-proxy range**: 2.35% (Apolo energies) to 5.42% (Sales Development 2)

**Key findings**:
- Active campaigns outperform inactive by ~32% on CPR (€0.0139 vs €0.0205)
- "Sales Development 2" is the benchmark campaign: highest volume + lowest CPR
- "Director Comercial" is the highest-value inactive campaign to reactivate (CPR €0.0109, 1,916 results)
- "Paid Media" had the largest budget but worst CPR — suspected misconfiguration
- Geographic experiment: "Campaing South Africa TECH" is atypical for LATAM-focused strategy

**Why:** First data baseline; use these numbers to contextualize future Meta campaign reports.
**How to apply:** When analyzing future Meta campaigns for Hutrit B2B, compare CPR against €0.016 blended benchmark. Flag anything above €0.022 as underperforming.
